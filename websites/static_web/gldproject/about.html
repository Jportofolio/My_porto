WHO ARE WE ? GLD Core-Services

GLD Projects is a dynamic management and project management company focusing on both public and private sector clients. Within the public sector we place special emphasis on the three spheres of government, namely National, Provincial and Local Government structures as well public entities.   GLD Projects was established in 2012 in response to a need for government practitioners who had an understanding and appreciation of the post 1994 challenges facing South Africa. The directors found a need for an empowerment company that could provide consulting and business advisory services particularly in the public sector sphere. This focus has led to a number of successfully completed assignments at National, Provincial and Local departments as well as State-Owned Enterprises. In today's knowledge driven, innovative and changing IT environment we offer our clients valuable counsel and practical solutions, enabling them to maintain a competitive edge through IT innovation, while fulfilling their strategic objectives.
